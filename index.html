Who Touched My Bible?!

KJV - King James Version

OT: Masoretic Text, some LXX and Vulgate influence.

NT: Textus Receptus, similar to the Byzantine text-type; some readings derived from the Vulgate.

Apocrypha: Greek Septuagint and Latin Vulgate.

ESV - English Standard Version

OT: Biblia Hebraica Stuttgartensia with Septuagint influence

NT: 83% correspondence to Nestle-Aland Novum Testamentum Graece 27th edition

Apocrypha: Göttingen Septuagint, Rahlf's Septuagint and Stuttgart Vulgate
